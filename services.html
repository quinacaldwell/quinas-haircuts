Our Services

At quinas mobile barbering, we offer a variety of hair and beard services such as haircuts and shaves, beard trims, and facial cleans. we also do locs, braids and womens and childerens haircuts and hairstyles.

We offer a range of services to meet your needs. Our prices vary depending on the type and duration of the service. Please refer to our pricing list below or visit our Setmore account to view detailed pricing information for each service.

To book an appointment, simply follow these steps:

  1. Select the service you're interested in.
  2. Choose a date and time that works best for you.
  3. Fill out the required information, including your name and contact details.
  4. Click "Book Appointment" to confirm your booking.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us. We look forward to serving you!
