About Us

We are a mobile barber business. We offer a variety of services such as men's haircuts, beard trims, and facial cleans. We also offer womens and children's haircuts and hairstyles. We also do locs, braids and facial cleaning. We hope you enjoy our haircutting and shaving services that we provide locally and mobile.


Experience and Qualification

Cutting and styling hair is my passion, I have been cutting hair since I was in high school. I would start by cutting my little brother's hair, my own hair, then my principal created an after school activity to allow me to practice cutting his beard, and other volunteers that wanted to get their hair cut by the high school barbers like me. After I graduated from high school, I moved on to getting my barber's license and I started my business shortly after I graduated. I also have a barber resume and portfolio to show that I am a qualified professional barber in the industry.
